VERTEX PROCUREMENT NETWORK PRIVACY NOTICE

Effective Date: January 9, 2026

Overview
Vertex Procurement Network provides business software and related support services. This notice describes how personal information is handled when customers, authorized users, website visitors, and business contacts interact with our services.


Information We Collect
We collect account registration details including name, business email address, company, role, and authentication identifiers. When customers use the service, we receive device identifiers, IP address, browser metadata, feature interaction events, support correspondence, and administrator configuration data. Customers may also upload business records for processing within their workspace.

How We Use Information
We use information to provide and secure the service, authenticate users, process customer instructions, troubleshoot incidents, respond to support requests, improve reliability and product features, communicate service changes, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations. Product analytics are used to understand adoption patterns and improve usability.

Retention
Account profile information is retained while the account is active and for up to 24 months after closure unless a longer period is required for legal claims, tax, fraud prevention, or security obligations. Support tickets are ordinarily retained for 36 months. Product telemetry is retained for 18 months. Backup copies age out according to documented backup rotation schedules.

Security
We maintain administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. Controls include role-based access, multifactor authentication for privileged users, encryption in transit, encryption at rest for production databases, vulnerability management, centralized logging, incident response procedures, and periodic access reviews.

International and Contractual Context
Where service delivery involves cross-border processing, contractual and technical measures are used according to applicable requirements and customer agreements. Enterprise customers remain responsible for configuring their workspaces and providing notices required for data they control.

Changes to This Notice
We may update this notice to reflect service, legal, or operational changes. Material revisions will be communicated through the service or other appropriate channels.
