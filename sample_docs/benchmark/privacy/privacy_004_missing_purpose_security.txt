ATLAS FLEET INTELLIGENCE PRIVACY NOTICE

Effective Date: January 4, 2026

Overview
Atlas Fleet Intelligence provides business software and related support services. This notice describes how personal information is handled when customers, authorized users, website visitors, and business contacts interact with our services.


Information We Collect
We collect account registration details including name, business email address, company, role, and authentication identifiers. When customers use the service, we receive device identifiers, IP address, browser metadata, feature interaction events, support correspondence, and administrator configuration data. Customers may also upload business records for processing within their workspace.

Sharing and Service Providers
We disclose information to contracted infrastructure hosting, customer support, email delivery, payment processing, observability, and security monitoring providers that act on our instructions. Information may also be disclosed when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or during a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets. We do not sell personal information.

Retention
Account profile information is retained while the account is active and for up to 24 months after closure unless a longer period is required for legal claims, tax, fraud prevention, or security obligations. Support tickets are ordinarily retained for 36 months. Product telemetry is retained for 18 months. Backup copies age out according to documented backup rotation schedules.

Your Privacy Choices and Rights
Subject to applicable law, individuals may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing. Requests may be submitted through the privacy request form or by email. We verify identity before fulfilling requests and respond within the period required by applicable law. Authorized agents may submit requests where permitted.

Contact
Privacy questions and requests may be sent to privacy@atlasfleet.example. Written correspondence may be directed to the Privacy Office, 450 Industrial Parkway, Detroit, MI. Complaints may also be raised through the customer support portal and will be routed to the privacy team.

International and Contractual Context
Where service delivery involves cross-border processing, contractual and technical measures are used according to applicable requirements and customer agreements. Enterprise customers remain responsible for configuring their workspaces and providing notices required for data they control.

Changes to This Notice
We may update this notice to reflect service, legal, or operational changes. Material revisions will be communicated through the service or other appropriate channels.
